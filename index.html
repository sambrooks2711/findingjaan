finding jaan

a lost note · a story we want to return

We found a handwritten note at Pudding Mill Lane station. It's folded into small squares, kept in a pocket for a long time. We want to find Jaan — to give back the note and listen to the story behind it.

✧ the note

My Love, Don't go!! We are going to miss you! It never get's easy :( Just want you to know that we LOVE you with all our heart and soul babey! Thank you for loving us the way you do and for taking care of us! No matter what, we appreciate you! If our words fail to show that, know that our hearts feel that! Wishing you a safe journey babey jaan! Love Luek and I xxx P.s. Chicken banana, chicken banana! lol
⚠️ The signature is unclear — "Luek" could be Luck, Lueh, Luch, or another variation. The note is from two people: Luek (or similar) and the writer ("I").
📅 dated october 2025 · found 26th april 2026 · 11am
📍 pudding mill lane station (near ABBA Voyage arena, London)
🌸 pink paper · torn edge · folded into perfect squares

📋 what we know

  • The note is addressed to someone named Jaan.
  • It was carried to London, likely by someone attending ABBA Voyage.
  • Written in English — the writer may be from Sweden / Scandinavia.
  • The note is from two people: Luek (or Luck/Lueh/Luch) + the writer.
  • The paper was folded small and kept in a pocket for months.

That's all. We don't know the story. We don't want to guess.

🎯 our goal

We want to give the note back to Jaan — or to the person who wrote it. We want to understand what happened. No judgement, no assumptions. Just the real story.

If you are Jaan, or if you recognise the note, the handwriting, the name, or anything at all — please reach out.
